## 1. Introduction

Locali ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

**App Provider:** C.H.A. LLC  
**Contact:** [support@cjhadisa.com](mailto:support@cjhadisa.com)  

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Phone Number:** Required for account registration and OTP authentication
- **Profile Information:** First name, last name
- **Neighborhood/Location:** Selected neighborhood for service discovery
- **Verification Documents:** Photos or PDFs uploaded for neighborhood verification
- **Booking Information:** Selected services, dates, times, and preferences
- **Payment Information:** Payment method details (processed securely via Stripe)
- **Messages:** In-app messages between users and service providers
- **Reviews & Ratings:** Feedback and ratings for completed bookings

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, time spent, navigation patterns
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs and app performance metrics
- **Location Data:** Approximate neighborhood location (derived from user selection, not GPS)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Stripe:** Payment processing details (we do not store full card data)
- **Supabase:** Authentication and session data

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Authenticate your account and provide app access
- Process and manage service bookings
- Facilitate in-app messaging between users and providers
- Process payments securely
- Send notifications about bookings and messages
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and ensure account security

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your information only:
- **With Service Providers:** Your profile and booking details are visible to service providers you book with
- **With Payment Processors:** Stripe processes payments securely (we do not store full payment card data)
- **With Cloud Providers:** Supabase (database) and Vercel (hosting) may access data as required to provide services
- **When Required by Law:** We may disclose information if legally required

**We do NOT sell or rent your personal information.**

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active; deleted upon request
- **Booking History:** Retained for 12 months for dispute resolution
- **Messages:** Retained for 6 months after booking completion
- **Payment Records:** Retained per financial regulations (typically 7 years)
- **Verification Documents:** Deleted after verification is complete

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Role-based access control in our database
- Secure password storage
- Regular security audits

**Limitation:** No security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable non-essential notifications

**To Exercise Rights:** Email [support@cjhadisa.com](mailto:support@cjhadisa.com)

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Locali is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.
