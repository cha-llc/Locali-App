## 🚀 STEP-BY-STEP FINAL SUBMISSION WORKFLOW

### STEP 1: Execute Android Build (20-30 minutes)
```bash
# From your machine:
eas build --platform android --profile production
```
**Status:** Ready to execute  
**Expected output:** APK/AAB file ~80-120 MB  
**Location:** https://expo.dev/builds

---

### STEP 2: Download APK/AAB (2 minutes)
1. Go to https://expo.dev/builds
2. Find "Locali" Android build
3. Click "Download"
4. Save file: `~/Downloads/Locali-1.0.0.aab`

---

### STEP 3: Create Google Play Console App (5 minutes)
1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Click "Create app"
3. Fill in:
   - **App name:** Locali
   - **Default language:** English
   - **App category:** Lifestyle / Shopping
   - **Type:** Free
4. Click "Create app"

---

### STEP 4: Upload APK/AAB (5 minutes)
In Play Console:
1. Left menu → "Release" → "Production"
2. Click "Create new release"
3. Upload APK/AAB file (Locali-1.0.0.aab)
4. Wait for verification (automatic, ~1-2 minutes)
